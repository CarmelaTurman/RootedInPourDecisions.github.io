Kare Kare

Kare Kare a pure Filipino tradition. This flavorful and hearty dish is made with tender oxtail, tripe and rich creamy and salty peanut-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs oxtail
  • 1 piece small banana flower buds sliced
  • 1 bundle pechay or bok choy
  • 1 bundle string beans cut into 2 inch length
  • 4 small - medium eggplants sliced 3/4 inch thick
  • 1 cup ground or whole peanuts
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste
  • 5 cups water
  • 4 cloves garlic chopped or minced
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic and onion powder
  • 1 packet Kare Kare mix
  • 3 slices of thick bacon, chopped in bitesize pieces
  • Optional: Red bell pepper for color pop
  • Optional garnish: 4 scallions, thinly sliced

Directions

  1. Rinse oxtail and pat dry. Season with salt, pepper, garlic and onion powder. Let sit for 10 min.
  2. In a hot skillet, sear oxtail on all sides. Set aside.
  3. In a large pot, brown bacon, add chopped onions and garlic until brown.
  4. Return oxtail in large pot, add water covering top of meat. Simmer for 1 to 1.5 - 2 hrs or until tender. Add more water as needed. (35 minutes if using a pressure cooker)
  5. Add the ground or whole peanuts, peanut butter, and Kare Kare mix. and simmer for 5 min.
  6. Once meat is tender, add vegetables and continue to simmer covered for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot over white rice. Top with 1/4 tsp shrimp paste and scallions for garnish.
